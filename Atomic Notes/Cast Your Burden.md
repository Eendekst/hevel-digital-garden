> "Throw your burden on Jehovah, and he will sustain you. Never will he allow the righteous one to fall." ([Psalm 55:22](https://www.jw.org/en/library/bible/nwt/books/psalms/55/))

The command is seemingly violent: **Throw it.**

Do not hand it over politely. Do not share it tentatively. **Cast it.**
