# Privacy Policy for Entretien Guillauto

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-22

## 1. Introduction
Entretien Guillauto ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Entretien Guillauto Operational Dashboard (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect information that allows us to manage our cleaning and maintenance operations, specifically:
*   **Customer Data:** Names, addresses, and service details synced from QuickBooks Online.
*   **Financial Data:** Invoice status and payment records synced from QuickBooks Online.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use this information solely for:
*   Internal operational management (scheduling, invoicing).
*   Business analytics and dashboarding.
*   **We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to outside parties.**

## 4. Data Security
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you access your dashboard. All data is processed via secure OAuth2 connections with Intuit.
