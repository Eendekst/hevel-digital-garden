# The Avatar: The Synthesizer

**The Synthesizer** is the target audience of **[[Hevel]]**.
They are not just users; they are architects.

### The Characteristics
1.  **Curiosity-Driven:** Insatiable need for the "Why."
2.  **Analytical:** Methodical mindset.
3.  **Creative:** Combining unrelated tools to solve novel problems.
4.  **Self-Directed:** They teach themselves because schools move too slow.

### The Feats
*   **Mastery:** They do not dabble; they conquer tools.
*   **Optimization:** They refuse defaults. They customize workflows.
*   **Systems Thinking:** They see the ecosystem, not just the app.
