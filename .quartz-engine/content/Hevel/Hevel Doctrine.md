# The Doctrine of Hevel

> *"The kingdom of the heavens is like a treasure hidden in a field..."* (Matthew 13:44)

**Hevel** is the blueprint for the Synthesizer.
It is a Christian organization empowering the curious to judge the world correctly.

## The Duality of Mastery
We do not choose between the Saint and the Soldier.

1.  **The Conviction of the Steward (The Why):** The anchor. The belief that talent is a sacred duty.
2.  **The Pragmatism of the Warrior (The How):** The engine. The ruthless execution required to win.
