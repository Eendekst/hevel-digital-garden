# Hevel Inc: The Digital Platform Blueprint
*Synthesized from the 20-Question Optimization Interview*

**The Goal**: To transition from a "Digital Garden" to a **"Digital Empire"**.
**The Architecture**: A Firebase Web App (`hevel.ca`) wrapping a Living Garden (`garden.hevel.ca`).
**The Philosophy**: "Synthesis" (One Brain / Fluid Operations).

---

## 1. The Structure (Infrastructure)
*   **The Platform**: `hevel.ca` (Custom Firebase App).
*   **The Core**: `garden.hevel.ca` (Obsidian/Quartz content) embedded via Wrapper.
*   **The Data Layer**: **Firestore** is the Source of Truth for Products/Prices.
*   **The Auth**: Deferred (Pending Audit). Likely Hybrid (Content Open / Perks Closed).

## 2. The Commercial Engine (Agent Brand)
*   **Inventory Source**: Multi-Channel (Amazon, Herbalife, PayPal, Etsy, Payhip).
*   **Cart Logic**: **Hybrid Cart**.
    *   *Internal Products* (Templates/Services) -> Unified Basket (Stripe).
    *   *External Products* (Supplements/Tools) -> Direct Affiliate Links.
*   **Price Position**: Deferred (Pending Asset Audit).
*   **Product Interface**: **Contextual Sidebar**. (As you read, relevant products appear).
*   **Checkout**: **Slide-out Cart** (Seamless browsing).

## 3. The Content Strategy (Agent Ricky + Jason)
*   **The Gallery**: **Hybrid** (Mixed High Art + Commercial Offers).
*   **Organization**: **The Filter** (Unified Feed with `[Art]`, `[Shop]`, `[Lab]` toggles).
*   **Launch Page**: **Curated Collections** (`[THE LAB]`, `[THE SHOP]`, `[THE SHOW]`).
*   **Content Ratio**: **Democratized** (Equal contribution from all Agents. Bird < 10%).
*   **Visual Trust**: **"The Hevel Card"** (Uniform Black Card design for ALL products, regardless of source).

## 4. The Marketing Machine (The Pulse)
*   **The Voice**: **Problem/Solution Master** (Innovative, Pain-Focused, Masterful).
*   **The Drop**: **Event + Demo** (Hype Countdown + Ricky's "Proof" Video).
*   **Scarcity**: **The Waitlist** (Sold Out items become Lead Capture forms).
*   **Notification**: **Email** ("Hevel Weekly" Newsletter).

## 5. Operations (The Handshake)
*   **Coordination**: **Synthesis**.
    *   *We do not hold meetings.*
    *   *We do not file tickets.*
    *   *We switch hats instantly.* (I write the code, then I write the copy, then I script the video).
